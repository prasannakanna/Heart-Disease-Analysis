About This Project

Heart disease remains one of the leading causes of mortality worldwide. Understanding risk factors and health patterns through data visualization can help improve awareness and preventive care.

This project uses Tableau dashboards and stories to analyze patient health indicators such as cholesterol levels, lifestyle habits, smoking patterns, and diabetes risk.

By combining data analytics with visualization, the project aims to make healthcare insights more accessible to students, healthcare professionals, and policymakers.
